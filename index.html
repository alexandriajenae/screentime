What Apps Do I Spend the Most Time On?

In the past 7 days, December 4th-December 11th, I have collected the data from my screen time on my phone. Using the average amount of hours per day that I am on each app I have created the following chart.

Chart outline by: IconFinder

Overall, I spend about 41 hours on my phone. My top apps are Snapchat, Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, Messages, GroupMe, and Twitter. My average daily pickups is 268 and I get an average of 402 notifications per day.

Snapchat is my main form of communication with my classmates and friends so it is no surprise that it is my highest used app. TikTok has been a great source of entertainment for me for the past few weeks and I usually spend around an hour every day before bed watching them to wind down. Twitter was surprisingly low to me because I feel like I spend more time on Twitter then GroupMe and Messenger at least so I did not realize it was actually lower.
